Common questions

How much do they cost?

Every extension is free to use. Some offer an optional Premium upgrade — $3 or $4 a month, depending on the extension — that unlocks extra limits or features. The price is always shown clearly before you pay, and it's never required.

How do I cancel Premium, or get a refund?

Premium is handled by ExtensionPay (using Stripe). You can cancel anytime, and it won't renew after that. For a refund or any billing question, just email support@edenapps.app and we'll sort it out.

Do they collect my data?

No. They run in your browser, store what they need on your device, and don't track you, run analytics, show ads, or sell anything. The full, per-extension detail is on the Extensions Privacy page.

I got a promotional email claiming to be from you — is it real?

If it's about paid "promotion," boosting installs, or improving your ratings, it isn't from us and isn't something we do or endorse. We only ever contact you from an @edenapps.app address. When in doubt, write to support@edenapps.app and ask.
