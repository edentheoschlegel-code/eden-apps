Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Eden Apps makes Snowball, Local PDF, Local Invoice, and LocalResume. This policy explains how we handle your information — and the short version is: we don't collect it.

Everything happens on your device

Our apps run entirely in your web browser. The documents, numbers, and text you enter are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to us or to anyone else. We have no servers that receive your content, because your content never leaves your browser.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, sign-ups, logins, or passwords.
  • No analytics, no tracking pixels, no advertising.
  • No tracking cookies and no third-party trackers.
  • We never sell, rent, or share your personal information — because we don't have it.

Information stored on your device

To make the apps useful, they save data in your browser's local storage — for example, the plan or document you're working on, and a record of a Pro upgrade you've purchased. This includes a randomly generated anonymous ID and a restore code, which contain no personal information. This data stays on your device, and you can clear it at any time through your browser settings or the app itself.

Payments

If you upgrade to Pro, the payment is handled by our payment provider, RevenueCat, using Stripe. You enter your payment details directly with them — we never see or store your card number. They process the transaction and keep records under their own privacy policies. We receive only an anonymous confirmation that a purchase was made; we do not receive your name, address, or card details from the app. See Stripe's Privacy Policy and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

If you email us

When you contact support, we receive your email address and message so we can reply. We use it only to help you and don't add you to any list.

Children

Our apps are general-purpose tools and are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them (and, as above, we don't collect personal information from anyone).

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@localinvoiceapp.com.
